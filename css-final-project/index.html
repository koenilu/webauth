Welcome to Mequon, Wisconsin!

Mequon is the largest geographical city in Wisconsin, and is home to over 27,000 people. Mequon is quite a fun place to be in. There is lots of things to do, ranging from good restaraunts, to cool locations. It is located directly off of Lake Michigan, so the location is quite cool. It also snows, so people from the south can experience massive amounts of snow, as well as below zero temperature. Additonally, you are within close distance of both Milwaukee, and not too far of a drive from Madison. There are lots of fun things you can also do in those locations. There is also a bike path that goes through Mequon, that will take you all the way to Milwaukee. Thiensville is also located within Mequon, which is a cute little town that is home to a very traditional little town, including more restaraunts, and interesting shops.
